KUK SCHOLARSHIPS - SCHOLARSHIP AND FINANCIAL AID

Kurukshetra University strongly believes in the fact that monetary constraints should not be an obstacle for a student to have access to quality education. So, the University extends Scholarship and Financial Aid Scheme which is an initiative by the University to cultivate the talent of students that may be hidden due to financial constraints. Scholarship/ Financial Aid can be availed in all the Programmes of the University, ranging from Diploma to Ph.D. In the last seven years, University has offered Scholarships/ Financial Aid worth 500+ Crores. Scholarship will generally be awarded on the basis of previous performance in academics, cultural, sports or other activities or under specific scholarship schemes category for a particular section of students. Financial Aid will be provided to the students belonging to underprivileged students like orphans, persons with disability etc. or some specific groups like Wards of Defence personnel etc

Scholarship Categories:

Scholarships are awarded to candidates based on their performance on any of the following categories:
1. Scholarship:
Eligibility Qualification/ Qualification previous to Eligibility Qualification:
The scholarship will also be offered on the basis of Eligibility qualification. For example, on the basis of performance in 10+2 for Undergraduate Programmes and Graduation for Postgraduate Programmes
2. Financial Aid:
~Financial Aid – Need Based
~Financial Aid for Serving/ Retired Defence/ CAPF/ Para- Military Personnel and their Dependents
~Financial Aid to Orphans
~Financial Aid to Persons with Certain Disability.

Scholarship Brackets:

For each category, there exists three brackets of Scholarship (Bracket 1, 2 and 3) are defined. Each bracket has its own respective criteria that need to be met for availing the applicable scholarship. Therefore, the students meeting the requirement of Bracket- 1 gets the highest Scholarship and those in Bracket - 2 or 3 get a relatively lesser scholarship.
Brackets Scholarship criteria (After Diploma and After 10+2 Programmes) Scholarship criteria (After Graduation Programmes) Bracket - 1
Students scoring 95% or above marks in qualifying exam or Qualification previous to eligibility qualification Students scoring 90% or above marks in Qualifying Exam or Students scoring 95% or above marks in Qualification previous to eligibility qualification.

Scholarship Phases:

Admissions in KUK are done in different Phases, which are bifurcations of the Admission time period. The amount of Scholarship also depends on the Phase in which the candidate takes admission in the University. (Phase – I) starts as early as January 2021 and continues till the end of April 2021. The subsequent phases (Phase-II, III, IV, V) are conducted only if the seats in the concerned Programme remain vacant. The amount of Scholarship reduces with each passing phase and no scholarship is offered in Phase - V (if conducted).

Scholarship can be availed on more than 150+ programmes, ranging from Diploma
Programmes to Post Graduation, offered by the University.
