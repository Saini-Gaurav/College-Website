APTITUDE SKILLS

Aptitude is a natural or acquired ability to do certain task easily and quickly. Such an assessment evaluates the talent/ability/potential to perform a certain task, with no prior knowledge and/or training.

SOFT & VERBAL SKILLS

Soft and Verbal Skills are personal attributes that play an important role in your life and are much sought after by the recruiters. Such skills enhance an individual's ability to get a job done and make you employable.

TECHNICAL SKILLS

Technical Skills help you to perform a technical or subject specific task. Such skills are practical, and often relate to mechanical, information technology, mathematical, or scientific domains.
