About Us

The Kurukshetra University was established in 1956
its campus is situated on the western bank of Brahm Sarover (the holy tank) and extends over an area
of over 400 acres. Starting with only the Department of Sanskrit,
it has grown into a multi-faculty University as one of the premier centres for advanced study and research in the region.
